MY HOBBIES

Writing Poetries is my favourite hobby. Whenever it feels like my heart is getting heavy so for me the best way to express my emotions and words in form of love and calmness.

Some Of my poetries are-

  1. Uthi jo ye kalam in haatho se tera naam likhe bagair ye rukti nahi
    Jo lafz nikle tere liye in hotho se tujhse pyaar ka izhar kiye bina ye ab thamte nahi.....
    Kafila jo nikla tha tere chahne walo ka Usme ek musafir mai bhi tha....
    chale jo mere kadam teri ore.... tum tak pahunche bagair ye thakte nahi.....

  2. Dil bhar gaya tera mujhse magar Ye din mera nhi guzarta
    Dil sambhalta hai badi mushkil se Ye ishq sambhalne nahi deta
    Raas nahi aa rhi zindagi tumhe ek pal ki bhi khushi
    Jo thaam lete ek baar haath mera puchkar mujhse mai mana nahi krta
    balki tumhe gale se laga leta

  3. Boht se manzar aaye boht se guzar gaye..
    hum waqt lautane aaye toh vo gunehgaar sudhar gaye...
    khuleaam rkhi thi apni kuch dil-Lagi maine...
    sambhalne kya lage thodi der ye mere dil-kash bikhargaye...
